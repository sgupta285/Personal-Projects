# Business Recommendation Memo

## Recommendation

Roll out the treatment in phases instead of globally on day one.

Start with:

- mobile sessions
- paid social and display traffic
- new and lapsed users

Keep the existing flow for the lowest-friction desktop and direct cohorts until a follow-up test confirms that the benefit is worth a full rollout.

## Reasoning

- the overall lift is positive
- the treatment directly improves the biggest leak in the funnel
- the strongest gains show up where friction is highest
- phased rollout preserves most upside while limiting downside risk
