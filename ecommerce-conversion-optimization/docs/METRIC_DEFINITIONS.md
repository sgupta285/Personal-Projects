# Metric Definitions

## Sessions at stage
Distinct sessions that reached a stage at least once.

## Conversion from previous
Sessions at current stage divided by sessions at the immediately previous stage.

## Dropoff from previous
One minus conversion from previous.

## Purchase conversion rate
Purchased sessions divided by total sessions in a variant.

## Absolute lift
Treatment conversion rate minus control conversion rate.

## Relative lift
Treatment conversion rate divided by control conversion rate minus one.

## MDE
Minimum absolute lift detectable with the configured sample size, alpha, and target power.
