# Measurement Assumptions

- Customer journeys are represented as ordered touchpoint sequences.
- Conversions occur at the journey level, not the touchpoint level.
- Touchpoint spend is observed for paid channels and zero for direct or organic traffic.
- Daily spend response curves are treated as separate planning inputs rather than direct causal truth.
- The optimizer keeps the total budget constant and only moves spend across channels.
- Bounds are set per channel to avoid unrealistic all-in reallocations.
- The project includes experiment hooks in the stakeholder summary to show how holdouts or geo tests could validate planning decisions later.
